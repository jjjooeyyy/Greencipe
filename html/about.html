Why Eat Out when you can eat in?

Here we cook and eat healthy and simple vegetarian food with natural ingredients, whole grains, good fats, fruit and vegetables.

We hope to inspire you with food that is both approachable AND exciting, whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, your roommates, or your friends.



our mission

In this space, we always sharing fresh, flavorful, (mostly) healthy recipes that we love to make and eat in every day life. If we wouldn’t eat it in real life, we won’t put in on the blog.

We would like to create and share recipes that feature real good, real food made for real people. Our recipes are fresh, seasonal, mostly simple, often healthy, and sometimes peppered with a little cheese and butter mixed in.

Where to start

2.Check out our recipe page that always use fresh ingredients, are mostly healthy, oftentimes seasonal, sometimes decadent.
